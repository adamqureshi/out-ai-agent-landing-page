FAQ

Quick answers for dealers.

How do you connect to our inventory?
We can ingest a CSV/XML feed or call an API endpoint you provide. The agent uses that data to recommend vehicles and answer availability/spec questions. Your team stays in control of what’s published.
Does it replace our salespeople?
No — it handles the repetitive questions and qualifies leads so your team spends time with serious buyers. You choose when OUT hands off to a human.
Can it help us buy cars from private sellers?
Yes. You can enable a “Sell my Tesla” flow that collects VIN, mileage, condition, photos (optional), and contact details. Your team can then follow up with an offer.
What about compliance and disclaimers?
You can configure disclaimers (pricing estimates, availability, financing terms). We recommend clear “subject to change” language and human review for final offers.
Can my developer host the backend on Azure?
Yes. This landing page is static. Your developer can keep the API, inventory sync, and lead routing on Azure. The widget can call your Azure endpoints.
